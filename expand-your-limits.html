Executive coaching

Our coaching is based on the Co-Active (CTI) model:

Coaching is an experience that provides awareness and drives change for the best, so coachees can reach their full potential.

 At we purpose we create the right space to make change happen for individuals and teams, we use a variety of training and coaching tools to give them the best experience.

We believe that everyone is naturally complete, creative and resourceful and we work with the person as a whole, focusing on his/her integral growth.

We help individuals to align with values and life purpose, becoming conscious of what makes this alignment happen.

We go along with each coachee to achieve a fulfilled life through conscious decisions and to achieve their individual and business goals.

We believe that this model allows coaches to sustainably integrate all the learning and use it effectively to impact the organization.

 

Team's coaching

None is an island, we’re all part of complex systems. A system is composed of many parts, yet the sum is greater than those parts.

Any issue in one individual impact the rest of the system, so working with the entire team we generate more impact than working with individuals.

We provide a vivid and practice experience. Using ORSC (Organization & Relationship Systems Coaching) tools to make the team become conscious and intentional in their relationships.

Team coaching creates a positive impact on the team itself and the entire organization.

A successful organization is composed of successful teams. We focus on the team’s potential empowering all team members, turning the entire group in a high-performance team
